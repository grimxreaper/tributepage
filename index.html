What You Didn't Know About Cleopatra

Here are some interesting facts about her life:

  • Cleopatra was not known for her unmatched physical appearance, as there were others in Egypt who were recognized as more physically attractive than she was. Yet, cleopatra was able to use many more of her traits to her advantage. One of them, being her voice, which was very enticicing and drew people in.
  • Marriage between brother and sister and father and daughter was a long-held practice in the Egyptian royal family. She was married to her two brothers.
  • People believed that she was the embodiment of the goddess Isis.
  • Cleopatra had a hand in the deaths of three of her siblings, which was not as unusual at the time.
  • She spent around 50% share of the kingdom income on beauty and other luxuries for herself. The other half she used for the kingdom.
  • Cleopatra Sailed and Fought Against the Roman Navy 31 BC!
